About us :

Welcome to KhanaKhazana, where we're all about the wonderful world of Indian vegetarian food. Our journey began in 2011, excited to share the fantastic flavors of Indian vegetarian cuisine with everyone.
At KhanaKhazana, our menu is full of tasty and diverse vegetarian options. From hearty and flavorful dishes to delightful bites, every plate carries the essence of India's diverse vegetarian culinary landscape.
Our team is made up of passionate chefs who love creating delicious food. Beyond serving great meals, we're also passionate about making choices that are good for our environment and community.
Come and visit us at KhanaKhazana to explore the delicious world of Indian vegetarian cuisine. We promise a dining experience filled with great food and good times.
